Hello, my name is
Will Lloyd. I'm a healthcare therapist turned UX Researcher and Designer.

Before venturing into the world of UX, I managed an outpatient physical therapy clinic and treated patients and private clients both.

My Journey

I am a UX designer, outdoor enthusiast and DIYer with a background as a healthcare therapist, manager, and strength coach. This skillset laid the foundation for a transition to UX design, giving me a unique insight into user-centered design from an empathetic background, informing design decisions for any project.

Lacking a real creative outlook in my work began to take a toll though and I found myself browsing more remote options to foster my creative talents. After finding the CareerFoundry UX Immersion course, I knew this would be something I could sink my teeth into and really get a start on design to change my career path. Though it was a decade in the healthcare industry, that time taught me about empathy, management, time-efficiency, Human Resources and a plethora of other skills that are challenging to gather throughout a career and life.
