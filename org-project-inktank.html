INKtank

Project Brief

Ink Tank users need an easy and efficient way to match with a potential tattoo artist who will work the best for them and find or expand upon their idea of what they want for a tattoo within a community of like-minded individuals who have a number of tattoos, are tattoo artists or are thinking of their first one. A community atmosphere will diminish the number of individuals who regret getting a tattoo or have a bad experience.

View Project on Behance.

Research

What new hypotheses do you have?

  • The augmented reality feature is not included in any of the content or screens. First it will need to be added as a recognizable icon on the screens with tattoos. Including the icon during the onboarding process will familiarize users with it when they see it throughout the app. A dedicated location within the app to access augmented reality will need to be added as well, likely under the profile or settings.

How will these hypotheses be validated?

  • After going through updates for the screens and content, more questionnaires and preference tests will be conducted using Usabilityhub and simple emailing and sharing the prototype.

Are there any areas of your product you believe need specific improvements?

  • The homepage and its content and how it is conveyed needs improvement as well as the whole onboarding process to allow users to skip or just continue as a guest to browse the content rather than having to sign-up. Although the goal is to get users to book appointments through the app, getting them into the app first would be more applicable and then having them create a profile/account prior to booking or during the booking process will hook them while theyâ€™re already invested.

How might your user testing evolve as you begin to polish your application?

  • User testing will involve to include more targeted demographics; tattoo enthusiasts, tattoo studios, tattoo artists, rather than the general populace which, while more people are getting tattoos, it will serve to get more concentrated insight from users who have tattoos and frequently get, give or browse tattoos. More specific navigation within the app will also be tested

What is your timeline for improvement and implementation?

  • The timeline will be a maximum of 4 weeks.

Ideation

A task analysis of how the user would navigate INKtank from sign up to artist selection for booking.

Wireframe

  • Design iterations were performed with the gathered information from usability testing, preference testing, visual design principles, design accessibility, and design collaboration.

  • Searching for an artist within the INKtank app needs to be quick, efficient and to the point so users will find what they're looking for and start the booking process for their first, third, tenth tattoo through our app as well.

  • The search process included an additional step while being wire framed which took the user longer to perform a search.

  • The extra step was removed in the high fidelity wire framing to minimize additional screens and navigation for the user, streamlining the search process and allowing for always-present filters across the top.
